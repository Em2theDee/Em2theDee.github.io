What is the New Britannic Script?

New Britannic is a modern script designed specifically for Standard British English; crafted to fit the language perfectly, like a glove, and eliminate the inconsistencies and frustrations of traditional English spelling. Say farewell to the confusion of words like through, rough, though, thought, tough, thorough, plough, bough, cough, enough, hiccough, and slough.
